Bring New Life To Strays!

By Angie Ortiz

Welcome To BNLTS!

The Mision:

Through my years of living in Puerto Rico, I've seen multiple strays on the loose in many commnunities. My mision is to help the stray animals in local communities find homes or shelters and encourage adopting strays and give them homes while I inform on the correct and safe ways to help these strays.

The Mission Declaration:

 Less strays being on the loose, safety for other animals and homes for the strays. It’s quite practical since it’s a win-win situation. The weakness could be that many won’t participate at first and that we can’t predict how strays will react to our sudden attempt to rescue them. Those who want to try and rescue the strays could risk getting hurt or attacked by them if they're not cautious. In order to counter this, I will post and spread word about my website and make known the proper precautions when addressing the strays. The strength is that there will be less strays on the loose and the pets that are outside in the front yards and the residents of the area are safe. We won’t have to continue cleaning up after them, we can sleep better at night, and we and our pets are safe.
